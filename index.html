Léo Planche

Current Occupation

I am currently working as a postdoctoral researcher at the NeuroMat Laboratory of the University of Sao Paulo. The project is about the modelization of neuronal activity via stochastic process on graphs.
Contact : leoplanche[at]gmail.com

PhD

I did my PhD in graph theory under the joint supervision of Etienne Birmelé, professor in Applied Mathematics at Université Paris Descartes, and Fabien de Montgolfier, maître de conférences (assistant professor) at Université Paris Diderot. My thesis (in french) is there.

Research topics

  • Graph theory and related algorithms
  • Statistics for networks
  • Stochastic Processes
